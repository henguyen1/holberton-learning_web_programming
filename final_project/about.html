I go to Lowell High School. The school colors are white and cardinal. The mascot is a cardinal. The school was founded under the name of Union Grammer School in 1856. The school then changed its name to Lowell High School in 1896. Lowell is the oldest high school west of the Mississipi River that is still in operation.
